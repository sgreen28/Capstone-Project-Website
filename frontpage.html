Making Shift Management Easier

The Voluntary Extra Time (VET) application is designed to simplify the process of signing up for extra shifts. By offering an intuitive and accessible platform, VET enables employees to volunteer for extra shifts, view detailed payment breakdowns, and easily manage their work-life balance.

With VET, employees are in control of their schedules, and employers can reduce administrative tasks related to shift management, enhancing overall business efficiency.
